Welcome to Moscow

More billionaires live there than any city in the world. Its current president’s popularity is measured against his predecessor’s by sales of their eponymous vodka brands. And contrary to popular belief, there are no bears!
Moscow is unparalleled if you are looking to “do-it-big” abroad. Russia’s capital city will entrance you with its luscious landscapes, breathtaking architecture, sumptuous dining, pulsating (often bacchanalian) nightlife and fascinating history.
If budgeted accordingly, you can enjoy 10 days indulging in all the world’s most expensive city has to offer for under $2,000.
I’ve done the research for you. This guide details the best lodging, dining, attractions, and clubs in Moscow for the traveler on a budget.
