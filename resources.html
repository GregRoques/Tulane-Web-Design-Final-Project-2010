Rescources

Here are some websites I found helpful when researching my trip:

Wikipedia: the most comprehensive overview of Moscow I found.

Lonely Planet : My favorite resource! Forget Fodors - if you want go beyond the typically tourist productions, eat where the locals eat, party where the locals party and experience the cultural zeitgeist, Lonely Planet will show you the road.

I highly suggest purchasing their book, Moscow City Guide, to take with you on your trip.

Wikitravel: Wikipedia offers a historic and cultural overview of the country; this site offers a brief and comprehensive guide for sight-seeing. It also provides a helpful paragraph on Russian etiquette, and staying healthy abroad.
